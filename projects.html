PollPlay


PollPlay is a Spotify-based song voting app. Users with Spotify-Premium accounts can create and host rooms. Rooms are accessible through the room code they choose. Upon entering a room, users can add songs and upvote existing songs. Songs will then be played through the host's Spotify account.

A Spotify premium account is required for this app.

Can You Spot The Fake?
  Click to Read!  

The above is a small demo version of my actual project! Articles are taken via the Reddit API. A random satire article is taken from the subreddit r/TheOnion which only allows posts with articles from Onion News (and its satirical sister sites). Another random article is taken from the subreddit r/nottheonion which mainly focuses on true stories that are so ridiculous that they are often thought to be fake news.

For the full game please click here for the Heroku link. For more information about my thought process and the problems and challenges faced while coding this please see my Github Repo.

Fake news itself is nothing new, but with the advent of new technology, the internet and social media has made it cheap and easy to perpetuate. While I do enjoy reading satire, the problem comes when one is unable to decipher between fact and fiction.

This project is a simple game in which two articles are pulled from the web, one being pure satire and the other, albeit real, has such bizarre facts which make it seem made up. Of the two articles, the goal is to spot the fake. You will be informed of which article is real or fake after making your choice and you would also be able to read the articles with the links provided. Happy reading!

Stack: Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL
